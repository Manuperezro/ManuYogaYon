FAQ

Quick answers about suitability and what to expect.

Is this for severe pain?

No—this is aimed at mild back discomfort/tension. If you have severe pain, sciatica symptoms, numbness, recent injury, or medical concerns, please consult a clinician first.

Do I need to be flexible?

No. We focus on safe mobility and relaxation, not extreme stretching.

How do I pay?

Reserve your place with the form, then pay at the studio on your chosen start date.

What should I bring?

Wear comfortable clothes. A mat is helpful. Props are optional: pillow, belt, two books as blocks.
