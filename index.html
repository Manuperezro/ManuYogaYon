FAQ

Quick answers.

Is this suitable for severe pain?

No—this is aimed at mild tension/discomfort only. If you have severe pain, numbness, recent injury, or medical concerns, check with a clinician first.

Do I need to be flexible?

No. We focus on safe mobility and relaxation—not extreme stretching.

How do I pay?

Reserve your place using the form, then pay at the studio on your chosen start date.

What should I bring?

Comfortable clothes. A mat if you have one. Optional props: pillow, belt, two books as blocks.
